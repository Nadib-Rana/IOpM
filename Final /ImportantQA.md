**Answer (English):**

   тЮд *Implication:* Inventory systems apply to goods, not services.

   тЮд *Implication:* Service systems must manage customer interaction.

   тЮд *Implication:* Need flexible processes in service delivery.

   тЮд *Implication:* Capacity planning is crucial in services.

   тЮд *Implication:* Service firms must use feedback tools.

6. **Labor Intensity** тАУ Services are more labor-intensive.
   тЮд *Implication:* Staff training is vital in service operations.
